COVID-19 affects different people in different ways.

Most infected people will develop mild to moderate symptoms.

Common symptoms:

  • fever.
  • tiredness.
  • dry cough

Some people may experience:

  • aches and pains.
  • nasal congestion.
  • runny nose.
  • sore throat.
  • diarrhoea.
